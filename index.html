Welcome to Yeshwant Mahavidhyalaya

Affiliated to Swami Ramanand Teerth Marathwada University, Nanded and recognized by UGC under 2(f) and 12(B). Yeshwant Mahavidyalaya, Nanded, was established in 1963 by Nanded Education Society.

Our institution stands as a beacon of learning, committed to providing quality education and fostering holistic development. We strive to create an environment where students can explore their potential and achieve excellence.
